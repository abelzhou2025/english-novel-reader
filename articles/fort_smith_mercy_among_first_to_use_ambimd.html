New AI system helps Mercy nurses spend more time with patients

正在翻译标题...
Mercy Hospital Fort Smith is one of the first health systems to use a new AI tool to reduce nurse documentation time. The Microsoft Dragon Copilot technology uses ambient AI to listen to patient interactions and automatically update health records. This tool aims to decrease nurse burnout by allowing more time for direct patient care. Early results show the AI has reduced documentation delays and saved nurses significant time per shift.
正在翻译...
Mercy Hospital Fort Smith is among the first health systems in the nation to use a new artificial intelligence tool designed to reduce the time nurses spend on documentation and increase the time they spend on direct patient care.
正在翻译...
The technology, known as Microsoft Dragon Copilot, uses ambient AI to capture and document patient interactions in real time. With patient consent, the system listens as nurses speak with patients, then automatically adds their observations into the patient's electronic health record.
正在翻译...
The tool is already in use in inpatient units at Mercy Hospital Fort Smith and at Mercy hospitals in St. Louis and Springfield, Missouri. Mercy Northwest Arkansas and additional Mercy locations are expected to adopt the technology in the coming months. Mercy is one of eight health systems nationwide that have worked directly with Microsoft on its development.
正在翻译...
Medical-surgical nurses across Mercy, including in Fort Smith, helped shape the tool by narrating their patient care so developers could refine how the system recognizes and records clinical details.
正在翻译...
The goal is to ease documentation demands that contribute to nurse burnout and staffing challenges, said Cheryl Denison, clinical integration director with Mercy's Office of Transformation.
正在翻译...
"We're seeing firsthand how Dragon Copilot is transforming the environment of care while also directly supporting nursing practice," Denison said. "By enabling nurses to document care more naturally through speech, ambient voice technology reduces cognitive load while also enabling them to do more of what they became a nurse to do in the first place --- care for patients."
正在翻译...
Ambient AI is technology that works quietly in the background, recognizing speech and activity in real time without requiring a device to be held or operated. In health care, ambient AI listens, with patient consent, as nurses and patients talk, then turns that spoken interaction into clinical documentation. The goal is to save time, reduce repetitive data entry, and allow medical staff to focus more on direct patient care.
正在翻译...
According to the World Health Organization, a global shortage of 4.5 million nurses is projected by 2030. Surveys show nurses spend more than a quarter of their shift on documentation and report increasing levels of stress and burnout. Mercy leaders say the new tool offers potential relief by streamlining routine tasks.
正在翻译...
Voice-based documentation: Nurses can narrate care and review AI-generated notes before they are filed in the electronic record. Quick access to clinical information: Nurses can retrieve reference material without switching between systems. Task automation: The system can help summarize patient interactions and draft documentation.
正在翻译...
Stephanie Whitaker, chief nursing officer at Mercy Hospital Fort Smith, said nurses using the system have noted stronger and more complete documentation while spending less time entering data.
正在翻译...
"Many of our nurses have said that by narrating as they provide care, which is pulled into the electronic medical record, they're noticing their documentation is much more robust," said Whitaker. "We cannot wait to roll it out across all of Mercy."
正在翻译...
Metrics provided by Microsoft show early results across Mercy include a 21% reduction in documentation delays, up to 24 minutes saved per shift for frequent users, a 29% drop in incremental overtime, and a 4.5% improvement in patient satisfaction.
正在翻译...
Umesh Rustogi, general manager with Dragon and Platform, Health and Life Sciences at Microsoft, added that feedback from Mercy's frontline staff was central to the system's development.
正在翻译...
"The real-time feedback from both the frontline nurses as well as the nursing informatics team at Mercy was really valuable in shaping this technology around real-world needs," Rustogi said.
