Insurance's new operating system for 2026: AI

finance.yahoo.com PR Newswire

Finance.yahoo.com 美通社

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

As the industry faces familiar challenges, experts foresee AI becoming the engine that drives the business

随着行业面临熟悉的挑战，专家预计人工智能将成为推动业务发展的引擎

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

CARY, N.C., Dec. 2, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- As insurers have entered the AI age, they've regarded the technology as something like a hot new phone app: They know they want it, but they are still figuring out the best way to use it. Looking ahead to 2026, SAS' industry experts foresee a breakthrough year in which AI will become central to how insurers operate -- no longer as an accessory, but as something like the business's operating system, powering functions from underwriting to claims decisions.

北卡罗来纳州卡里，2025 年 12 月 2 日 /美通社/ -- 随着保险公司进入人工智能时代，他们将这项技术视为一种热门的新手机应用程序：他们知道自己想要它，但他们仍在寻找使用它的最佳方式。展望 2026 年，SAS 的行业专家预计，人工智能将成为突破性的一年，人工智能将成为保险公司运营的核心——不再是一个附件，而是像企业的操作系统一样，为从承保到理赔决策的各种功能提供支持。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Even as the industry is poised to embrace this technological transformation, though, it continues to face growing challenges from climate change, economic turbulence and regulatory volatility that could threaten the future of insurance.

尽管该行业已做好迎接这一技术变革的准备，但它仍然面临着气候变化、经济动荡和监管波动带来的日益严峻的挑战，这些挑战可能威胁到保险业的未来。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

There are still a few weeks to go before the new year, but there's no need to wait for a slow restart. Let's boot up those industry forecasts from SAS experts now:

距离新年还有几周的时间，但没必要等待缓慢的重启。现在让我们启动 SAS 专家的行业预测：

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

A Fortune 500 insurer will begin phasing out policy admin systems in favor of insurance copilots in 2026. Some large insurers have already signaled their intent to invest big in AI technologies. And SAS' survey data shows that insurance executives have a high level of trust in generative AI -- twice as high as for machine learning, in fact. Policy admin systems require substantial investment and upkeep. However, interactions with data through copilots can eliminate the need to utilize those admin systems to underwrite policies or settle claims.

一家财富 500 强保险公司将于 2026 年开始逐步淘汰保单管理系统，转而采用保险副驾驶。一些大型保险公司已经表示有意在人工智能技术上进行大量投资。SAS 的调查数据显示，保险高管对生成式人工智能的信任度很高，实际上是机器学习的两倍。策略管理系统需要大量投资和维护。然而，通过副驾驶与数据交互可以消除利用这些管理系统来承保保单或解决索赔的需要。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

-- Franklin Manchester, Principal Global Insurance Advisor

——富兰克林·曼彻斯特，首席全球保险顾问

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Many straightforward insurance claims will be settled in minutes by agentic AI.In order to safeguard customers' trust, though, insurers will need strong AI governance. That means ensuring that their AI platform has the security controls and governance to minimize risks, from accidental bias in claims decisions to exposure to cyberattacks. The companies that install robust AI governance will earn and protect that trust. Building systems that act fast -- and act right -- will define the leaders of the next decade.

许多简单的保险索赔将通过代理人工智能在几分钟内解决。不过，为了维护客户的信任，保险公司将需要强大的人工智能治理。这意味着确保他们的人工智能平台具有安全控制和治理，以最大限度地降低风险，从索赔决策中的意外偏见到遭受网络攻击。实施强大的人工智能治理的公司将赢得并保护这种信任。构建快速且正确行动的系统将定义下一个十年的领导者。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

-- Alena Tsishchanka, Global Customer Advisory Director

——Alena Tsishchanka，全球客户咨询总监

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Insurers will lean harder into AI-powered actuarial modeling and decisioning. This will result in improved accuracy, speed and efficiency across the policy life cycle, from underwriting through claims. The opportunity is twofold: meaningful progress in narrowing the industry's $1.8 trillion protection gap and greater resilience in the face of escalating climate risk and economic volatility.

保险公司将更加倾向于人工智能驱动的精算建模和决策。这将提高整个保单生命周期（从承保到索赔）的准确性、速度和效率。机遇是双重的：在缩小行业 1.8 万亿美元的保护缺口方面取得有意义的进展，并在面对不断升级的气候风险和经济波动时增强抵御能力。
