YouTube Now Shutting Down Channels Posting AI Slop

futurism.com Frank Landymore

futurism.com 弗兰克·兰迪莫尔

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

YouTube's janitors are swooping in to mop up the slop.

YouTube 的清洁工正在冲进来清理污水。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

As reported by Deadline, the Google-owned video platform has terminated two massive YouTube channels that peddled fake, AI-generated movie trailers, in what is one of the most high profile actions it's taken against the AI spam polluting the platform.

据 Deadline 报道，谷歌旗下的视频平台已经终止了两个大型 YouTube 频道，这些频道兜售人工智能生成的虚假电影预告片，这是针对污染该平台的人工智能垃圾邮件采取的最引人注目的行动之一。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

"The monster was defeated," one YouTuber told Deadlineafter the slop channels were taken down.

“怪物被打败了，”一位 YouTuber 在 slop 频道被关闭后告诉 Deadline。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Whatever your thoughts on AI imagery, these channels weren't using the tech to innocently envision some fanboy's dream casting. They were unquestionably farming engagement by using AI shots spliced together with actual copyrighted movie footage to trick viewers, per Deadline , and then posting the fake trailers early to drown out the real ones. In March, for example, Screen Culture churned out an outrageous 23 different trailers for "The Fantastic Four: First Steps," with some of them outranking the official trailer, Deadlinefound.

无论你对人工智能图像有何看法，这些频道并没有利用这项技术来天真地想象一些粉丝的梦想选角。毫无疑问，他们通过使用人工智能镜头与实际受版权保护的电影镜头拼接在一起来欺骗观众，然后提前发布假预告片以淹没真实的预告片，从而吸引观众参与。例如，今年 3 月，Screen Culture 为《神奇四侠：第一步》制作了 23 个不同的预告片，其中一些预告片的排名甚至超过了官方预告片《Deadlinefound》。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Why is YouTube banning them now? The channels, apparently, had gotten cocky. YouTube had quietly suspended their ability to make ad revenue after a Deadlineinvestigation earlier this year, but were able to claw back ad money after adding "fan trailer," "parody," and "concept trailer" to their video titles. But, for reasons unknown, both slop slingers stopped including those disclaimers in recent months, thus inviting the ban hammer.

为什么 YouTube 现在禁止它们？显然，频道已经变得自大了。YouTube 在今年早些时候的 Deadline 调查后悄悄暂停了广告收入，但在视频标题中添加了“粉丝预告片”、“戏仿”和“概念预告片”后，得以收回广告收入。但是，由于未知的原因，近几个月来，两家污水抛投者都停止了包含这些免责声明，从而招致了禁令锤子。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The decision comes as AI continues to make inroads into the actual movie industry --- and as AI-spoofed trailers become a hot button issue. Users of OpenAI's video generating app Sora have been churning out loads of AI-spoofed Disney trailers, including a Pixar-style animation about Jeffrey Epstein inviting children to his "amazing" island --- a trend that didn't seem to bother Disney when it announced it would invest $1 billion into OpenAI and officially license its characters for Sora last week.

这一决定是在人工智能继续侵入实际电影行业之际做出的，而且人工智能欺骗的预告片已成为一个热点问题。OpenAI 的视频生成应用程序 Sora 的用户一直在制作大量人工智能欺骗的迪士尼预告片，其中包括一部有关杰弗里·爱泼斯坦 (Jeffrey Epstein) 邀请孩子们去他的“神奇”岛屿的皮克斯风格的动画——当迪士尼上周宣布将向 OpenAI 投资 10 亿美元并正式为 Sora 授权其角色时，这一趋势似乎并没有困扰迪士尼。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

What this means for the rest of all the other slop polluting YouTube is unclear. The platform is still overrun with AI-generated music, hours-long informational videos presented in a "boring" or "sleepy" style, and even veritable snuff films. Some are using AI to impersonate public figures, garnering hundreds of thousands of views. What probably happened here is that the AI movie trailer channels simply played fast and loose with copyright laws for too long, ticked off the wrong IP owners, and got kicked to the curb. And good riddance.

这对于 YouTube 上其他所有污水污染意味着什么尚不清楚。该平台仍然充斥着人工智能生成的音乐、以“无聊”或“昏昏欲睡”风格呈现的长达数小时的信息视频，甚至是名副其实的鼻烟电影。有些人利用人工智能冒充公众人物，获得了数十万次观看。这里可能发生的情况是，人工智能电影预告片频道在版权法上玩得太久了，惹怒了错误的知识产权所有者，然后被踢到了路边。祝你摆脱困境。
