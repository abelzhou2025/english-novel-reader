Are you listening to bots? Survey shows AI music is virtually undetectable

www.reuters.com Jaspreet Singh

www.reuters.com 贾斯普雷特·辛格

Nov 12 (Reuters) - A staggering 97% of listeners cannot distinguish between artificial intelligence-generated and human-composed songs, a Deezer--Ipsos survey showed on Wednesday, underscoring growing concerns that AI could upend how music is created, consumed and monetized.

路透 11 月 12 日 - Deezer 与益普索 (Ipsos) 周三进行的一项调查显示，惊人的 97% 的听众无法区分人工智能生成的歌曲和人类创作的歌曲，这突显出人们越来越担心人工智能可能会颠覆音乐的创作、消费和货币化方式。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The findings of the survey, for which Ipsos (ISOS.PA), opens new tab polled 9,000 participants across eight countries, including the U.S., Britain and France, highlight rising ethical concerns in the music industry as AI tools capable of generating songs raise copyright concerns and threaten the livelihoods of artists.

Ipsos (ISOS.PA) 打开了新选项卡，对美国、英国和法国等 8 个国家的 9,000 名参与者进行了调查，结果突显了音乐行业日益增长的道德担忧，因为能够生成歌曲的人工智能工具引发了版权问题并威胁到艺术家的生计。

It also showed that most listeners want clear labelling on AI-generated music, music streaming platform Deezer (DEEZR.PA), opens new tab said.

这还表明，大多数听众希望在人工智能生成的音乐上有清晰的标签，音乐流媒体平台 Deezer (DEEZR.PA) 打开新标签说。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The study found that 73% of respondents supported disclosure when AI-generated tracks are recommended, 45% sought filtering options, and 40% said they would skip AI-generated songs entirely. Around 71% expressed surprise at their inability to distinguish between human-made and synthetic tracks.

研究发现，73% 的受访者支持在推荐人工智能生成的歌曲时进行披露，45% 的受访者寻求过滤选项，40% 的受访者表示他们会完全跳过人工智能生成的歌曲。大约 71% 的人对自己无法区分人造轨道和合成轨道表示惊讶。

"We believe strongly that creativity is generated by human beings, and they should be protected," Deezer CEO Alexis Lanternier told Reuters, urging transparency.

Deezer 首席执行官亚历克西斯·兰特尼尔 (Alexis Lanternier) 告诉路透社，“我们坚信创造力是人类产生的，他们应该受到保护”，并敦促透明度。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Lanternier noted the complexity of implementing differential payout structures for AI music, stating a "massive change" in remuneration policies remains challenging. Deezer has also started excluding fake streams from royalty payments.

Lanternier 指出了为人工智能音乐实施差异化支付结构的复杂性，并表示薪酬政策的“巨大变化”仍然具有挑战性。Deezer 还开始从版税支付中排除假流。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The issue gained attention earlier this year when AI band "The Velvet Sundown" attracted one million Spotify (SPOT.N), opens new tab listeners monthly before its synthetic origins were exposed.

今年早些时候，这个问题引起了人们的关注，当时人工智能乐队“The Velvet Sundown”吸引了 100 万 Spotify (SPOT.N) 的关注，在其合成来源曝光之前每月都会打开新的标签听众。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Universal Music Group (UMG.AS), opens new tab recently settled a copyright case with AI music company Udio. While financial terms were undisclosed, the parties plan to launch an AI-powered music creation platform in 2026, using licensed music to train the tool.

环球音乐集团 (UMG.AS) 最近与人工智能音乐公司 Udio 解决了版权案。虽然财务条款尚未披露，但双方计划于 2026 年推出人工智能驱动的音乐创作平台，使用授权音乐来训练该工具。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

On Tuesday, a Munich court ruled that OpenAI's ChatGPT violated German copyright laws by reproducing song lyrics, a decision the company said it might appeal.

周二，慕尼黑一家法院裁定 OpenAI 的 ChatGPT 复制歌词违反了德国版权法，该公司表示可能会对这一裁决提出上诉。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Consumer attitudes toward AI in media remain mixed. A May survey by Luminate found the majority of U.S. audiences were indifferent or accepting of AI use in cinema tasks like visual effects, but sceptical of AI-written scripts or synthetic actors.

消费者对媒体人工智能的态度仍然参差不齐。Luminate 5 月份的一项调查发现，大多数美国观众对人工智能在视觉效果等电影任务中的使用漠不关心或接受，但对人工智能编写的剧本或合成演员持怀疑态度。
