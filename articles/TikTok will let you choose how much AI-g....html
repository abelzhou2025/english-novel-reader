TikTok will let you choose how much AI-generated content you want to see

techcrunch.com Aisha Malik

techcrunch.com 艾莎·马利克

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

TikTok, an app that was once just a place for user-generated content, is launching a new setting that lets users choose how much AI-generated content they want to see in their For You feed. The company is also introducing more advanced labeling technologies for AI-generated content.

TikTok 是一款曾经只是用户生成内容的应用程序，它正在推出一项新设置，让用户可以选择他们希望在 For You feed 中看到多少人工智能生成的内容。该公司还为人工智能生成的内容引入了更先进的标签技术。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The new AI-generated content (AIGC) control is rolling out within the app's Manage Topics tool, which lets users choose what they see on TikTok.

新的人工智能生成内容（AIGC）控件正在该应用程序的管理主题工具中推出，该工具允许用户选择他们在 TikTok 上看到的内容。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

"Manage Topics already enables people to adjust how often they see content related to over 10 categories like Dance, Sports, and Food \& Drinks," TikTok explained in a blog post. "Like those controls, the AIGC setting is intended to help people tailor the diverse range of content in their feed, rather than removing or replacing content in feeds entirely."

TikTok 在一篇博客文章中解释说：“管理主题已经使人们能够调整他们看到与舞蹈、体育和食品饮料等 10 多个类别相关的内容的频率。”“与这些控件一样，AIGC 设置旨在帮助人们定制提要中的各种内容，而不是完全删除或替换提要中的内容。”

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The move comes as companies like OpenAI and Meta are embracing AI-only feeds. In September, Meta released Vibes, a new feed for sharing and creating short AI-generated videos. A few days after Meta's launch, OpenAI released Sora, a social media platform for creating and sharing AI-generated videos.

此举出台之际，OpenAI 和 Meta 等公司正在拥抱纯人工智能提要。9 月，Meta 发布了 Vibes，这是一个用于分享和创建人工智能生成的短视频的新源。Meta 推出几天后，OpenAI 发布了 Sora，一个用于创建和分享人工智能生成视频的社交媒体平台。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Since Sora's launch, realistic AI-generated videos have been posted to TikTok. Additionally, many TikTok users are leveraging AI to create visuals for posts about other topics, like history or celebrities.

自 Sora 推出以来，由人工智能生成的逼真视频已发布到 TikTok 上。此外，许多 TikTok 用户正在利用人工智能为有关其他主题（例如历史或名人）的帖子创建视觉效果。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

TikTok says that with the new AI-generated content control, users who want to see less of this sort of content can now dial things down, while those who enjoy it can choose to see more of it.TikTok

TikTok 表示，通过新的人工智能生成的内容控制，想要减少此类内容的用户现在可以减少观看次数，而那些喜欢此类内容的用户可以选择查看更多此类内容。 图片来源：TikTok

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

You can access the new capability by going into Settings > Content Preferences > Manage Topics. Then you can move the slider for different topics, including AI-generated content, to adjust how much of that content you want to see in your For You feed.

您可以通过进入“设置”>“内容首选项”>“管理主题”来访问新功能。然后，您可以移动不同主题（包括 AI 生成的内容）的滑块，以调整您希望在“为您推荐”源中看到的内容量。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

The change is rolling out in the coming weeks, TikTok says.

TikTok 表示，这一变化将在未来几周内推出。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

To improve its ability to label AI-generated content, TikTok is now testing a technology called "invisible watermarking."

为了提高标记人工智能生成内容的能力，TikTok 目前正在测试一种名为“隐形水印”的技术。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

TikTok already requires people to label realistic AI-generated content and uses a cross-industry technology called Content Credentials from C2PA, which embeds metadata into content that lets it and other platforms know when something is AI-generated. However, TikTok notes that these labels can be removed when content is re-uploaded or edited on other platforms.

TikTok 已经要求人们标记真实的人工智能生成的内容，并使用 C2PA 的一种名为“内容凭证”的跨行业技术，该技术将元数据嵌入到内容中，使其和其他平台知道某些内容何时是人工智能生成的。然而，TikTok 指出，当内容在其他平台上重新上传或编辑时，这些标签可以被删除。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

With the new "invisible watermarks," TikTok will add another layer of safeguards by using a watermark that only it can read. That means it'll be harder for others to remove it.

借助新的“隐形水印”，TikTok 将通过使用只有它才能读取的水印来增加另一层保护措施。这意味着其他人将更难删除它。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

TikTok will start adding invisible watermarks to AI-generated content made with TikTok tools like AI Editor Pro. It's also adding them to content uploaded with C2PA's Content Credentials. The company says these watermarks will help it label content more reliably. TikTok notes that it will continue reading C2PA's Content Credentials and adding them to AI-generated content made on its platform.

TikTok 将开始向使用 AI Editor Pro 等 TikTok 工具制作的人工智能生成内容添加隐形水印。它还将它们添加到使用 C2PA 的内容凭证上传的内容中。该公司表示，这些水印将帮助其更可靠地标记内容。TikTok 指出，它将继续读取 C2PA 的内容凭证，并将其添加到其平台上制作的人工智能生成的内容中。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Related to these efforts, TikTok also announced that it's launching a $2 million AI literacy fund aimed at experts, like the nonprofit Girls Who Code, to create content that teaches people about AI literacy and safety.

与这些努力相关的是，TikTok 还宣布将启动一项 200 万美元的人工智能素养基金，旨在帮助非营利组织 Girls Who Code 等专家创建内容，向人们传授人工智能素养和安全知识。
