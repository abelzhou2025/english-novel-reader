God is now just one text away because of AI

theweek.com Devika Rao, The Week US

theweek.com Devika Rao，《美国周刊》

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

They say God is always with you, and now that includes in your pocket. From chatbot Jesus to AI-written sermons, churches are using the technology to try to get more people engaged with religion. AI could improve access and allow pastors more freedom for hands-on work, but it may not be effective in drawing in the masses.

他们说上帝永远与你同在，现在这也包括在你的口袋里。从聊天机器人耶稣到人工智能撰写的布道，教堂正在利用这项技术试图让更多的人参与宗教活动。人工智能可以改善接触机会，让牧师有更多的自由来开展实际工作，但它可能无法有效吸引群众。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Mass media

++AI++ is also being used to convey otherworldly messages. The ++technology++ allows people the "feeling they are talking to a divine power, clergy member or deceased person," said Axios. For example, the app Text With Jesus lets users chat with and ask questions of Jesus. The app quotes the Bible and seems to provide thoughtful responses. Still, with apps like these, "we have no idea what's under the hood there, what's really creating the reality that then they present," said Robert P. Jones, a religious researcher, to ++Today++.

++AI++ 也被用来传达超凡脱俗的信息。Axios 表示，++技术++让人们“感觉自己正在与神圣力量、神职人员或死者交谈”。例如，“Text With Jesus”应用程序可让用户与耶稣聊天并向耶稣提问。该应用程序引用了圣经，似乎提供了深思熟虑的回应。尽管如此，对于这样的应用程序，“我们不知道它们的底层是什么，也不知道是什么真正创造了它们所呈现的现实，”宗教研究员罗伯特·P·琼斯(Robert P. Jones)对++Today++说。

Some pastors have said they use AI to draft sermons for their congregations. Many argue that "AI sermons not only draw on a wealth of sources, but also leave more time for pastoral care," said Deena Prichep in NPR's ++Weekend Edition Saturday++. The "goal of a sermon is basically to tell a story that can break open the hearts of people to a holy message. So does it matter where that comes from?" One church in Phoenix, Arizona, played an AI-generated message from Charlie Kirk from beyond the grave, in which he said that his "soul is secure in Christ."

一些牧师表示，他们使用人工智能为会众起草讲道。许多人认为，“人工智能布道不仅利用了丰富的资源，而且还为教牧关怀留下了更多时间，”Deena Prichep 在 NPR 的++周末版星期六++中说道。“布道的目标基本上是讲述一个故事，可以打开人们的心，接受神圣的信息。那么它来自哪里重要吗？”亚利桑那州凤凰城的一座教堂播放了一条由人工智能生成的来自查理·柯克在坟墓之外的信息，他在信息中说他的“灵魂在基督里是安全的”。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

New blood

Denominations of Christianity are not the only religions that have integrated AI into their sermons or practices. There are also "Buddhist, Hindu, Jewish and Islamic chatbots, but some religions are more open to adopting new technologies than are others, and for different uses," said Brian Owens at ++Nature++.

基督教教派并不是唯一将人工智能融入其布道或实践的宗教。++Nature++ 的布莱恩·欧文斯 (Brian Owens) 表示，还有“佛教、印度教、犹太教和伊斯兰教的聊天机器人，但有些宗教比其他宗教更愿意采用新技术，而且用途不同。”

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Adults who are ++religiously unaffiliated++, meaning they identify as atheists, agnostics or as "nothing in particular," make up approximately 29% of the population, said ++Pew Research Center++. But embracing AI technology could attract more people to religion. "Culture responds to that new technology and there are new standards or practices that emerge," said Brad Hill, the chief solutions officer of faith-based AI platform Gloo, to ++Christianity Today++. "People who are in the business of flourishing and people who are trying to advance good need to be equipped with the very best tech so that they can apply it to that end."

++皮尤研究中心++表示，++无宗教信仰++的成年人，即他们认为自己是无神论者、不可知论者或“没什么特别的”的人，约占人口的 29%。但拥抱人工智能技术可以吸引更多人信仰宗教。基于信仰的人工智能平台 Gloo 的首席解决方案官布拉德·希尔 (Brad Hill) 对 ++今日基督教 ++ 表示：“文化对新技术做出了反应，并出现了新的标准或实践。”“从事繁荣事业的人们和努力推进美好事业的人们需要配备最好的技术，以便他们能够将其应用到这一目的。”

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

AI bots and other tools are "addressing an access problem," said ++The New York Times++. Many people have "longed for spiritual guidance, and have had to travel, sometimes great distances, to reach spiritual leaders." Now, "chatbots are at a user's fingertips." However, using AI to spread religious messages "might not be as effective and convincing or inspirational" as "putting a person in the role of a religious authority," said Owens.

++《纽约时报》++ 表示，人工智能机器人和其他工具正在“解决访问问题”。许多人“渴望精神指导，不得不长途跋涉，有时甚至是很远的距离，才能接触到精神领袖。”现在，“聊天机器人触手可及。”然而，欧文斯表示，使用人工智能传播宗教信息“可能不如“让一个人扮演宗教权威的角色”那样有效、令人信服或鼓舞人心。
