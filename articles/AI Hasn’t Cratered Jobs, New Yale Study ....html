AI Hasn't Cratered Jobs, New Yale Study Says

www.usnews.com By Olivier Knox|Nov. 12, 2025

www.usnews.com 作者：奥利维尔·诺克斯|十一月2025 年 12 日

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Regular readers of this column know that I'm something of a "doomer" when it comes to artificial intelligence. It's making it increasingly easy to dupe people online. It's accused of encouraging young people to kill themselves. It may make millions of jobs obsolete.

本专栏的老读者都知道，在人工智能方面，我是一个“厄运者”。这使得在网上欺骗人们变得越来越容易。它被指控鼓励年轻人自杀。它可能会使数百万个工作岗位变得过时。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

Closer to home, it's cratering traffic to news sites, with AI-pocalyptic ramifications for outlets trying to stay afloat.

言归正传，新闻网站的流量大幅下降，人工智能对试图维持生计的媒体产生了灾难性的影响。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

So it was with a mix of surprise and something like relief that I read a new study from the Budget Lab at Yale University that argues AI has not led to vast economy-wide negative effects. Yet.

因此，当我读到耶鲁大学预算实验室的一项新研究时，我感到既惊讶又松了口气，该研究认为人工智能并未对整个经济产生巨大的负面影响。然而。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

"Overall, our metrics indicate that the broader labor market has not experienced a discernible disruption since ChatGPT's release 33 months ago, undercutting fears that AI automation is currently eroding the demand for cognitive labor across the economy," the report says.

报告称：“总体而言，我们的指标表明，自 ChatGPT 33 个月前发布以来，更广泛的劳动力市场并未经历明显的破坏，这削弱了人们对人工智能自动化目前正在侵蚀整个经济体对认知劳动力需求的担忧。”

The Caveats

There are two broad caveats.

有两个广泛的警告。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

In other words, just because the report did not find sweeping effects from AI to date, that doesn't mean that AI won't eventually bring about those kinds of changes. The Budget Lab also noted that the pace of adoption of AI has been inconsistent, even among sectors that are vulnerable to replacement.

换句话说，仅仅因为该报告迄今为止没有发现人工智能的全面影响，并不意味着人工智能最终不会带来此类变化。 预算实验室还指出，即使在容易被替代的行业中，人工智能的采用速度也不一致。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

"For instance, generative AI tools were adopted extremely quickly and at mass scale among coders and software developers, who are in the top quintile of exposure," it said. "Meanwhile, adoption has lagged considerably in clerical sectors, despite a similar level of exposure."

报告称：“例如，生成式人工智能工具在程序员和软件开发人员中得到了极快和大规模的采用，他们的曝光率处于前五分之一。”“与此同时，尽管接触程度相似，但文职部门的采用却相当滞后。”

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

But the Budget Lab's conclusion is fairly upbeat for now.

但预算实验室的结论目前相当乐观。

[翻译占位 - 翻译占位符]

"While anxiety over the effects of AI on today's labor market is widespread, our data suggests it remains largely speculative," the report said. "While generative AI looks likely to join the ranks of transformative, general purpose technologies, it is too soon to tell how disruptive the technology will be to jobs."

报告称：“虽然人们普遍担心人工智能对当今劳动力市场的影响，但我们的数据表明，这在很大程度上仍然是推测性的。”“虽然生成式人工智能看起来有可能加入变革性通用技术的行列，但现在判断该技术将对就业产生多大的颠覆性还为时过早。”
